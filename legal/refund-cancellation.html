Refund & Cancellation Policy

Clear, fair, and transparent terms for project changes and cancellations.

1. Project initiation

Engagements commence upon acceptance of our proposal and receipt of the initial payment or purchase order, as applicable.

2. Cancellations

You may cancel with written notice. Billable time, third‑party costs, and completed milestones up to the date of notice remain due.

3. Refunds

  • Discovery/strategy fees are non‑refundable once work has begun.
  • For fixed‑scope projects, unused prepaid amounts after deducting delivered work may be refunded.
  • Retainers and ongoing services are non‑refundable for the current billing period.

4. Change requests

Scope changes are handled through change orders with updated estimates and timelines.

5. Timelines

Client delays may shift schedules. We’ll provide revised timelines and dependencies.

6. Contact

Questions? Email contact@msdigitalworld.in.
